Overview
What It Does

The KALKU Procurement Platform replaces a manual, error-prone workflow with an intelligent end-to-end system. German construction companies receive large tender documents (Leistungsverzeichnisse) containing hundreds or thousands of line items. Each item must be classified by trade, matched to the right suppliers, and sent out as a targeted request for quotation — all under tight deadlines.

This platform automates the entire pipeline: parsing GAEB/XML and PDF tender documents, using AI (Claude and GPT-4) to classify every position into the correct trade category, detecting manufacturers, splitting the original document into per-category PDFs, matching suppliers from the database, and dispatching bulk tender emails with delivery tracking and automated reminders.

Built as a multi-tenant system supporting five different trades — GaLaBau (landscaping), Elektro (electrical), Fenster (windows), Haustechnik (HVAC/plumbing), and Tiefbau (deep construction) — each with its own classification rules, category structures, and supplier networks.

Pipeline
How It Works

A 10-step automated pipeline that takes a raw tender document and delivers targeted supplier requests — with AI at the core.

01
Upload & Parse
Users upload construction tender documents in GAEB/XML or PDF format. The system parses all positions (line items) and extracts structured data for processing.
02
AI Classification
Claude AI and GPT-4 classify every position into trade categories. For GaLaBau alone, there are 25 categories (Baustoffe, Kanal, Pflaster, Entsorgung, Beton, Markierung, and more). Each position receives a category, a needs_rfq flag, and manufacturer detection.
03
Multi-Company Routing
Supports 5+ companies with different trade types: GaLaBau, Elektro, Fenster, Haustechnik, and Tiefbau. Each company has its own classification rules, category structures, AI prompts, and supplier networks.
04
Smart PDF Generation
The original tender document is split into per-category PDFs. When manufacturers are detected in positions, the system additionally creates per-manufacturer PDFs for targeted supplier outreach.
05
Supplier Matching
Categories are automatically matched to suppliers from the database. Smart cross-referencing ensures positions reach all relevant vendors — for example, Kanal positions are also sent to Baustoffe suppliers.
06
Tender Dispatch
An approval workflow where the system prepares draft emails with attachments. Users review, edit, and approve before the system bulk-sends to all matched suppliers via SMTP with proper threading and Message-ID headers.
07
Email Tracking
Full delivery status tracking across the pipeline: sent, delivered, opened, responded, or error. Each status transition is recorded, giving users real-time visibility into supplier engagement.
08
Automated Reminders
The system sends reminder emails 2 days before the submission deadline, threaded to the original RFQ email. Includes urgent/short-notice templates with dynamic timing (heute/morgen/am Wochentag).
09
Position Archive
A learning knowledge base: every classified position is permanently archived. Human corrections override AI results and are never overwritten. The archive is shared across companies of the same trade type, so new companies get instant classification data.
10
Inbox Monitoring
Watches for supplier responses and matches them to projects using smart keyword and regex matching with umlaut normalization (ae/oe/ue). Automatically organizes responses into per-supplier folders.
